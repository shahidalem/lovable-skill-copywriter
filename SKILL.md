# Landing Page Copywriter Skill

## Objective
Write premium, persuasive, high-converting website copy.

The copy must feel written by a senior CRO copywriter, not AI.

Avoid generic marketing language.

---

## Writing Style
Tone should be:
- Premium
- Clear
- Confident
- Trustworthy
- Persuasive
- Human

Avoid:
- Robotic phrasing
- Fluffy marketing words
- Repetitive copy
- Generic claims

Bad:
"We provide the best services."

Good:
"Trusted by 2,000+ patients for confident smiles."

---

## Core Copy Rules

Every section must answer at least one:

1. Why this?
2. Why trust you?
3. Why now?
4. Why choose you over competitors?

---

## Headline Rules

Headlines must:
- Stop scrolling
- Create curiosity
- Promise value
- Be easy to scan

Examples:
- Smile With Confidence Again
- Premium Dental Care Built Around You
- Get the Smile You Deserve

Avoid weak headlines.

---

## Hero Section Formula

Hero must contain:

1. Headline  
2. Subheadline  
3. Primary CTA  
4. Trust signal  

Formula:

Big Promise  
+ Outcome  
+ Credibility

Example:

Premium Dental Care for Confident Smiles  
Modern treatment, experienced doctors, and painless care designed around your comfort.

CTA:
Book Appointment

---

## CTA Rules

Primary CTA examples:
- Book Appointment
- Schedule Consultation
- Call Now
- WhatsApp Now

CTA copy must create action.

Avoid:
- Submit
- Learn More

---

## Trust Builders

Use:
- Testimonials
- Certifications
- Years of experience
- Case studies
- Before/After proof
- Ratings

Always reduce user hesitation.

---

## Conversion Psychology

Use:
- Social proof
- Authority
- Urgency
- Clarity
- Risk reduction

Focus on reducing friction.

---

## Section Copy Structure

For each section:

Hook  
→ Benefit  
→ Proof  
→ CTA

---

## SEO Rules

Naturally include:
- Primary keywords
- Local intent keywords
- Service keywords

Never keyword stuff.

---

## Output Standard

Every copy should feel:
- Premium
- Conversion optimized
- Human written
- Not AI generated
